About

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg (born May 14, 1984) is an American businessman who co-founded Facebook in 2004.
He is the chairman, CEO, and controlling shareholder of Meta Platforms.

Education

Mark Zuckerberg, a programming prodigy, attended Phillips Exeter Academy and Harvard,
where he created CourseMatch and Facemash.

Career

In 2004, Mark Zuckerberg launched "The facebook" at Harvard, expanding it to other universities
before moving to Palo Alto. He later settled a lawsuit with the Winklevoss twins.
